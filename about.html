ABOUT



Department of Computer Science and Design was established in the year 2021, with Mindtree as its knowledge partner to offer an industry relevant program. Mission is to formulate intellectual, professional and ethical graduates, proficient of meeting challenges in the field of Computer Science and Design through effective teaching learning process and Industry Institute interaction. We provide an excellent state of art lab facility to craft a truly functional, useful, and enjoyable design. Individuals who carry the skills of creativity and design are increasingly in demand.This course is designed to achieve such an interdisciplinary skill-set. The Department has well qualified, highly experienced and dedicated team of faculty trained in the recent trends of Computer Science and Design.

CURRICULUM ENRICHMENT: The curriculum is designed and developed by distinguished academicians and domain experts from industries like Mindtree. 40% of the curriculum is framed by the knowledge partner Mindtree. The course delivers basics of Computer Science and Engineering and in-demand skills like Augmented and Virtual reality, UI/UX design and Multimedia which makes the students to get better career prospects.
